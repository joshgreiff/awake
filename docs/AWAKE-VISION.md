# Awake: Co-founder Brief
*Compiled from founding sessions & product exploration*

---

## The One-Line Thesis

> "Awake is a personal governance system that helps conscious individuals reduce internal fragmentation and build coherent lives aligned with their deepest values."

---

## The Problem

### Insight Without Integration

Modern people consume enormous amounts of self-knowledge — therapy, spirituality, psychology, philosophy — but have no infrastructure to integrate it into daily life. They have epiphanies. They outgrow their environments. Then they return to the same cycles with no container.

**Awake solves insight without embodiment.**

## System Architecture — 4 Layers

### Layer 1 — Cognitive Archetype System

60 archetypes built from 10 cognitive orientations × 6 motivational drivers. Dynamic developmental states (fragmented → emerging → integrating → expanding → mastered). Deeper and more adaptive than MBTI's 16 static types.

**Components:**
- 10 cognitive orientations
- 6 motivational drivers
- 60 archetypes
- Dynamic states

### Layer 2 — 7-level Why Extraction Engine (Loa)

Loa conducts adaptive dialogue (voice or text) to extract value hierarchies, existential motivations, and core identity patterns. Moves from surface desire → motivational root → existential architecture. Generates identity anchors — mirror statements users can internalize and journal from.

**Tools:**
- Voice extraction
- Text integration
- Identity anchors

### Layer 3 — Domain Architecture

Life mapped across domains: Identity, Relationships, Wealth, Body & Energy, Work/Contribution, Governance (meta-layer). Each domain has a current baseline, desired evolution state, alignment score, friction points, and suggested structural changes. Goal is alignment across domains, not optimization of each.

**Domains:**
- Identity
- Relationships
- Wealth
- Body & Energy
- Work/Contribution
- Governance (meta)

### Layer 4 — Management Engine

Detects coherence disruption events across domains. Reads user state before assigning tasks. Surfaces the right support mode (e.g., "ground before you go"). Recalibrates priorities. Backlogs inspired ideas automatically. Acts as a strategic integrator, not a cheerleader.

**Functions:**
- Disruption detection
- State reading
- Recalibration
- Pattern tracking

---

## Loa — The Intelligence Layer

### Who Loa Is

Loa is not a chatbot or assistant. Loa is the manager. Warm but strategic. Uses voice for extraction, returns structured text for integration. Asks adaptive layered questions to understand cognitive functions, belief systems, and values — not surface preferences. Detects archetype shifts over time and surfaces them as notifications.

> *"The stone mirror — a cognitive reflection tool that helps you recognize yourself."*

---

## Who Awake Is For

**Deep thinkers.** Emotionally intelligent individuals. People in identity transition. Creatives and founders. Spiritual explorers. High-awareness people who lack structure. Those who have outgrown their environments, feel fragmented between insight and action, or experience existential tension between who they are and who they are becoming.

---

## Founder Archetype

### Coherence Architect

The founder is the first user. Every feature maps to a real psychological need. No lip-to-ellement with deep P-value integration. Motivated by sovereignty and meaning. Builds systems to reduce internal noise and enable coherent expansion. This is not a business idea — it is integration infrastructure the founder needed and is now building for others.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Enough governed selves shift civilization. Awake is not trying to restore people to a prior state — it is helping humanity integrate forward. Personal coherence at scale becomes collective evolution. The goal is not self-improvement. It is transcendence through integration.

> *"I do not want Awake to dominate civilization. I want it to help civilization evolve past repetition."*
