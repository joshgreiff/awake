# Awake V2: The Consciousness Game

**Mission:** Help neurodivergent individuals go from "lost in space" to becoming conscious authors of their own lives through gamification.

---

## The Core Framework

Based on Dan Koe's "Fix Your Life in 1 Day" protocol, adapted for gamification.

### The Six Components (Your Game Elements)

| Component | Game Term | What It Is |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **Anti-Vision** | The Nightmare | Life you NEVER want - what happens if nothing changes |
| **Vision** | The Dream | Ideal life you're building toward |
| **1-Year Goal** | The Mission | ONE thing that makes this year a success |
| **1-Month Project** | The Boss Fight | This month's big focus/project |
| **Daily Levers** | Daily Quests | 3-5 actions that move the needle every day |
| **Constraints** | The Rules | What you're NOT doing (creates focus) |

## The Game Structure

### Chapter System (Life Stages)

Your life is divided into chapters. Each chapter is ~3-6 months.

```
Chapter 1: Lost in Space
- You're stuck, overwhelmed, directionless
- Tutorial mode: Learn the basics
- Goal: Complete your first vision + anti-vision

Chapter 2: Finding Direction  
- You have clarity but building momentum
- Unlock: Boss fights, constraints
- Goal: Complete first monthly boss fight

Chapter 3: Building Momentum
- Taking consistent action, seeing results
- Unlock: Advanced insights, community features
- Goal: 30-day streak, level 10+

Chapter 4: Conscious Author
- You're designing your life intentionally
- Unlock: Mentor mode, create challenges for others
- Goal: Complete yearly mission
```

## The Vision System (Redesigned)

### Creation Flow (One-Time, ~30 min)

**Step 1: The Nightmare (Anti-Vision)**
> "Describe your life in 6-12 months if NOTHING changes. Be brutally honest. What does a typical Tuesday look like? How do you feel? What opportunities have you missed?"

Purpose: Create emotional stakes. This is what you're fighting AGAINST.

**Step 2: The Dream (Vision)**  
> "Now describe your ideal life in 6-12 months. Same level of detail. What does that Tuesday look like? Who are you becoming? How do you feel?"

Purpose: Create emotional pull. This is what you're fighting FOR.

**Step 3: The Mission (1-Year Goal)**
> "What's the ONE thing that, if accomplished this year, makes everything else easier or unnecessary? Make it measurable with a deadline."

Example: "By December 31, 2025, AnyLingo has 100 paying customers."

**Step 4: The Boss (1-Month Project)**
> "What's THIS month's big project? What's the one thing you'll complete that moves you toward the mission?"

Example: "Launch AnyLingo public beta by January 31."

**Step 5: Daily Weapons (Daily Levers)**
> "What 3-5 daily actions, if done consistently, guarantee progress? These become your default quests."

Examples:
- Morning reflection (non-negotiable)
- 2 hours deep work on boss fight
- 30 min exercise
- No social media before noon
- Bed by 11pm

**Step 6: The Rules (Constraints)**
> "What are you NOT doing this month? What distractions are you eliminating? Constraints create focus."

Examples:
- No new projects until boss is defeated
- No Netflix on weekdays
- No phone in bedroom
- Only check email 2x/day

## Reflection System (Redesigned)

### Morning Check-In (2 min)
Quick, low friction:
- Energy level (1-10 slider)
- One word for how you feel
- Review today's quests
- See vision/anti-vision reminder

### Evening Reflection (5-10 min)
Deeper conversation with LOA:

**Pattern Interruption Questions** (randomized):
- "What did you avoid today?"
- "When did you feel most alive?"
- "What would someone conclude about your goals based on your behavior today?"
- "Did you move toward the dream or the nightmare?"

**Synthesis Questions**:
- "What worked today?"
- "What's one thing you'd do differently?"
- "What's the smallest version of your best self you could be tomorrow?"

**Output**:
- XP awarded based on quest completion
- Boss damage calculated
- Tomorrow's quests generated
- Trait XP distributed

## Key Principles

1. **Stakes matter** - Show both dream AND nightmare. Motivation needs both carrot and stick.

2. **One boss at a time** - Focus is everything for neurodivergent brains. One monthly project.

3. **Constraints = freedom** - Rules make the game fun. Limiting options reduces overwhelm.

4. **Quick wins daily** - Morning check-in should take < 2 min. Low barrier to start.

5. **Progress is visible** - XP, levels, boss damage, streaks. The brain needs to SEE progress.

6. **Failure is feedback** - Miss a day? Boss heals a bit. Don't defeat boss? Learn and adjust. Never punish harshly.
